Orthodent

DENTAL CLINIC WEBSITE • 2024

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Orthodent is a modern dental clinic website designed to provide patients with easy access to dental services information and online booking capabilities. The site features a clean, professional design that instills trust and confidence while making it simple for patients to learn about services and schedule appointments.

Built with patient experience in mind, the website includes detailed service descriptions, doctor profiles, an interactive gallery showcasing successful treatments, and a streamlined appointment booking system. The responsive design ensures accessibility across all devices.

KEY FEATURES

  • Online appointment booking system
  • Detailed service pages with pricing information
  • Doctor profiles and credentials
  • Before/after treatment gallery
  • Patient testimonials and reviews
  • Multi-language support (Bulgarian/English)
  • Contact forms and live chat integration
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • SEO optimized for local search
